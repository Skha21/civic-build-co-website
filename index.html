Why councils tender to us

Small enough to be accountable. Organised enough to deliver.

We built Civic Build Co around the way public-sector procurement actually works — clear scopes, documented compliance, and a director on site, not just in the office.

    Direct director involvement

    Every project is overseen personally by one of our three directors — no layers between the tender and the trowel.

    SWMS, WHS records and council reporting are built into how we run a job, not bolted on afterward.

  • 03
