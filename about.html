About Us

A new company, built on real experience.

Civic Build Co Pty Ltd was founded in 2026 by three directors who set out to build a construction company purpose-fit for the way councils and government agencies actually procure work.

Our Story

Formed with a clear purpose

Civic Build Co is a newly established company. Saad Khan, Chris Gatt and Erkan Erten came together to form a dedicated construction business rather than continuing to operate independently — combining their individual backgrounds in construction, civil works and compliance under one company built specifically to tender for and deliver local council and government contracts.

As a new entity, Civic Build Co is building its own project history from the ground up. The experience our directors bring individually — including hands-on renovation trade work across bathrooms, kitchens, laundries and full home projects, alongside civil, remedial and safety compliance experience — is what stands behind every tender we submit, and every job we take on.

What We Stand For
  • H
