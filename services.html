Capability Statement

Scoped for tenders. Built for standards.

Our capability is organised the way a council or government scope of works is written — by discipline, with clear reference codes for easy matching against tender documents.

CIV—01

Civil & Remedial Works

Site remediation and earthworks, footpath and kerb & guttering renewal, minor road and pathway repair, and general civil infrastructure works for council-owned land and facilities.

PLB—02

Plumbing & Drainage

Stormwater drainage, sewer connections and repairs, and plumbing works for public facilities, amenities blocks, parks and council buildings.

DEM—03

Demolition & Site Prep

Small-to-medium scale demolition, site clearing and preparation ahead of council or government redevelopment works.

MNT—04

Facility & Asset Maintenance

Ongoing and reactive maintenance works for council-owned buildings, community facilities and public infrastructure assets.

CON—05

Concrete & Structural Works

Concreting, footings, retaining structures and general structural works to support council and state infrastructure projects.

TND—06

Tendered Panel Contracts

Delivery under standing offer arrangements and panel contracts for local councils and state government agencies, including small-business set-aside programs.

How We Work With Tenders

A straightforward process, start to award.

  • 01

    Scope review

    We read every tender scope closely and ask questions early, rather than guessing at intent.

  • 02

    Clear, compliant submission

    Pricing, methodology and compliance documents are prepared to match exactly what's been requested.

  • 03

    Director-led delivery

    If awarded, a director is directly accountable for the job from mobilisation to sign-off.
